# MCA – Company / LLP CIN Lookup API

## 📘 What is MCA – Company / LLP CIN Lookup?

The **Corporate Identity Number (CIN)** is a 21-character alphanumeric identifier assigned to Indian companies and LLPs by the **Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA)** through the **Registrar of Companies (ROC)**.

The **CIN Lookup API** enables users to retrieve a company’s or LLP’s **CIN based on the entity name**, making it ideal for business verification, onboarding, compliance, and due diligence processes.

---

## ✅ Benefits of MCA CIN Lookup API

1. 🏢 **Instant CIN Discovery**  
   Retrieve Corporate Identity Numbers (CINs) using the legal company or LLP name.

2. 🔍 **KYB Enablement**  
   Supports Know Your Business (KYB) workflows with structured, verifiable data.

3. 🛡️ **Fraud Mitigation**  
   Prevents onboarding of illegitimate or unregistered businesses.

4. ⚙️ **Compliance Acceleration**  
   Speeds up audits, legal vetting, and onboarding processes by linking to verified MCA records.

## 💼 What Can You Do with This Product?

- Lookup CIN or LLPIN using a company or LLP’s official name  
- Integrate into onboarding, contract validation, or vendor compliance workflows  
- Enable KYB/AML checks for regulated industries or partnerships
